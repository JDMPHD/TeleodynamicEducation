## **1.6 QUALITY STANDARDS RECAP:**

### **Glimpses (from 1.6):**

* **Always:** "It is \[year\]. You are..." format (present tense, second person)  
* **Length:** 1-3 paragraphs (typically 1-2)  
* **Purpose:** Immersive sensory experience, make reader inhabit a moment  
* **No explanation:** Just experience, visceral and immediate  
* **Specific details:** Names, places, concrete objects  
* **Emotional resonance:** Not just facts, but felt experience

**Example from 1.6:**

"It is 1351\. The monk with the red headband is drawing a circle in the dirt with a stick..." "It is 1415, and you are seven years old. Your father has brought you to Nanjing to see the procession..."

### **Zoom-Ins (from 1.6):**

* **Third person, present tense**  
* **Extended scenes** (multiple paragraphs, building momentum)  
* **Deep interiority:** Character's thoughts, memories, sensations  
* **Psychological complexity:** Not just what happens, but what it means to the person  
* **Sensory richness:** Smells, textures, sounds embedded throughout  
* **Transformation or realization:** Character understands something by the end  
* **Historical texture:** Specific details that ground the scene in time/place
